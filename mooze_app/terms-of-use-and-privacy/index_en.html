A) Acquisition of Digital Assets via Mooze

All acquisitions of digital units carried out through Mooze are governed by these Terms of Use, which regulate the software intermediation services for transmission of digital units. Any acquisition of assets made through fiat currency payments to global partners of the Eulen.app network, through Banking as a Service (BaaS) in Brazil, refers exclusively to DEPIX – synthetic unitary digital token partially collateralized in fiat currencies and mostly in Bitcoin, through on-chain networks and/or the Liquid Network, as described by the issuing entity at Depix.info in its public whitepaper.

When making the fiat payment in the Brazilian financial system, the user acquires DEPIX in the name and ownership of Mooze, acknowledging that the assets belong to Mooze at the time of the transaction. The user expressly accepts this condition by proceeding with the operation or by accepting these Terms of Use. Thus, the payment made to a BaaS partner is used to acquire DEPIX on behalf of Mooze.

This DEPIX acquisition contract in the name of Mooze allows the user to select, on the platform, two distinct contractual modalities, each with specific service provision, as detailed in the following items.

B) Acquisition of Digital Units: DEPIX

When selecting the DEPIX option on the platform, the user contracts the service of acquiring DEPIX in the name and ownership of Mooze. The assets are kept under Mooze’s possession to enable the execution of intermediation services through automated software. These services include addressing the DEPIX to a self-custody wallet of the user, whose address is automatically captured by Mooze’s systems through seeds or seed phrases previously registered by the user and which Mooze will never know, have knowledge of or access to.

In this modality, the user acquires the service of organization, addressing and orchestration of transactions on a large scale. Mooze acts as a bridge between the DEPIX token issuer and BaaS partners, using automated anti-fraud verification systems, hash generation and asset addressing. The objective is to ensure system scalability for the end user, through the purchase of DEPIX in the name of Mooze.

The operation may take a few minutes or hours, especially if anti-fraud systems identify suspicious behavior, such as attempts of unauthorized access to systems, endpoints or PIX transactions that may harm BaaS partners of the Eulen.app network. After completion of the verifications, Mooze deducts its service fee (related to software and automations) from the amount to be delivered. Only then are the DEPIX issued in favor of the user, becoming their ownership when transferred in a unidirectional and irreversible manner to their self-custody wallet via previously registered or created seed phrases on the platform by the user and which Mooze will never know and has no knowledge or access to.

If irregularities or failures are detected in the transaction of the DEPIX acquired in the name of Mooze, the assets will be returned to the issuer for reversal. In this case, the fiat payment will be refunded to the user through the PIX system, via BaaS partners of the Eulen.app network, with the consequent cancellation of the contract and the service provided.

C) Acquisition of Digital Units: Bitcoin

When selecting Bitcoin (on any blockchain network available on the platform), the user contracts the service of acquiring DEPIX in the name and ownership of Mooze. The acquired DEPIX are used by Mooze to redeem, in a decentralized manner, the majority collateral of the token – Bitcoin – in its country of operation. According to the public DEPIX whitepaper, the backing in Bitcoin is structured through NITI protocols (Non-custodial Interlinked Tokenization Infrastructure), allowing redemption via Liquid Network or Lightning networks, as detailed at Depix.info in its public whitepaper.

In this modality, the user delivers DEPIX in the ownership of Mooze to enable the redemption of the collateral in Bitcoin. Mooze applies automated anti-fraud verification systems, addressing and orchestration of transactions on a large scale, acting as a bridge between the DEPIX issuer and BaaS partners. The service ensures scalability of addressing to the end user.

The operation may take a few minutes or hours, especially in case of detection of suspicious behavior by anti-fraud systems. After completion, Mooze deducts its service fee from the amount to be delivered. Only then is Bitcoin issued in favor of the user, becoming their ownership when transferred in a unidirectional and irreversible manner to their self-custody wallet via previously registered or created seed phrases on the platform by the user and which Mooze will never know and has no knowledge or access to.

If irregularities or failures are detected in the transaction of the DEPIX acquired in the name of Mooze, the assets will be returned to the issuer for reversal. In this case, the fiat payment will be refunded to the user through the PIX system, via BaaS partners of the Eulen.app network, with the consequent cancellation of the contract and the service provided.

Both contractual modalities involve the charging of fees for the use of Mooze’s transaction facilitator systems, including options optimized for merchants and direct or indirect provision of APIs or endpoints that interconnect DEPIX services to BaaS or EaaS systems (Exchange as a Service via NITI protocols only) of the token issuer.

Mooze makes access to acquisition services available exclusively through the Brazilian financial system, using PIX via BaaS technological bridges. Only individuals with active Brazilian citizenship, holders of a bank account in Brazil and bearers of a national identification document can use the payment services. This restriction stems from legal requirements for operations in the Brazilian financial system.

Explanation:

- BaaS (Banking as a Service): Platform that allows non-banking companies to offer financial services – such as accounts, payments and transfers – through APIs, without the need for their own banking license, provided they operate under applicable regulatory authorizations.

- EaaS (Exchange as a Service): Model that provides infrastructure for cryptoasset exchanges via APIs, allowing the creation of personalized platforms with a focus on compliance, liquidity and integration. In the case of Mooze and DEPIX, the NITI protocol is used (example: Sideswap and its public API).
